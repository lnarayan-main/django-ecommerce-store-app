Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

At MyApp, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal data such as name, email address, and profile image during registration.
  • Usage data, including login activity, browser type, and device information.
  • Uploaded media files such as photos or videos linked to your account.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including:

  • Authenticating and managing user accounts.
  • Processing uploads and managing media content securely via Cloudinary.
  • Sending notifications or updates related to your account.

3. Data Security

We store media files securely in Cloudinary and ensure your data is encrypted in transit. Only authorized personnel can access sensitive user data for maintenance or support purposes.

4. Your Rights

You may access, update, or delete your personal data by logging into your account or contacting support.

5. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@myapp.com
