Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I create an account?

Click the Register button on the homepage and fill in your name, email, and password. Once registered, you can log in immediately after account activation.

2. How can I upload my profile picture?

Go to your Profile page and click “Edit Profile.” You can upload a new image — we store it securely using Cloudinary.

3. Where is my data stored?

Your profile information is stored in our PostgreSQL database, while images and videos are hosted on Cloudinary’s secure servers.

4. I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login page, click “Forgot Password?” and follow the instructions to reset your password through the email link.

5. How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team by emailing support@myapp.com.
